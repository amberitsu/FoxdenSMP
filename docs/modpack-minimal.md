# Minimal
The minimal variant which includes the minimal mods required to connect to the server

**World**
- [BetterEnd](https://modrinth.com/mod/betterend), new End biomes
- [BetterNether](https://modrinth.com/mod/betternether), new Nether biomes
- [Ribbits](https://modrinth.com/mod/ribbits), adds Swamp villages with musical frogs
- [Naturalist](https://modrinth.com/mod/naturalist), adds new animals

**Farming and Cooking**
- [Animal Feeding Through](https://modrinth.com/mod/animal_feeding_trough), mobs will feed themselves using this
- [Create Slice & Dice](https://modrinth.com/mod/slice-and-dice), automate the production of Farmer's Delight food
- [Create Food](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-food), expands the food options from Farmer's Delight
- [Farmer's Delight](https://modrinth.com/mod/farmers-delight-refabricated), adds a new cooking system alongside a variety of meals

**Storage**
- [Applied Energetics 2](https://modrinth.com/mod/ae2), advanced storage system that converts matter into energy and vice versa
- [Create Contraption Terminals](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-contraption-terminals), integrates Tom's Simple Storage Terminals into Create contraptions
- [Tom's Simple Storage Mod](https://modrinth.com/mod/toms-storage), simple vanilla style storage mod
- [Traveler's Backpack](https://modrinth.com/mod/travelersbackpack), upgradeable backpack

**Tech**
- [CC Tweaked](https://modrinth.com/mod/cc-tweaked), adds programmable computers, turtles and more to the game
- [Create (Refabricated)](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-fabric), large tech mod that adds many new ways to experience the game
- [Create Cobblestone](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-cobblestone), add cobblestone generators to Create
- [Create Copper and Zinc](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-copper-zinc), adds renewable sources of copper and zinc to Create
- [Create Crafts & Additions](https://modrinth.com/mod/createaddition), acts as a bridge between electricity and kinetic energy from Create, as well as integrates CC Tweaked and Applied Energetics 2 into Create.
- [Create New Age](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-new-age), adds nuclear reactors and many more sources of energy
- [Create Steam 'n' Rails](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-steam-n-rails), expands Create's railway options and logistical systems

**Aesthetic**
- [Create Bells & Whistles](https://modrinth.com/mod/bellsandwhistles), adds decorative blocks to trains
- [Create Deco](https://modrinth.com/mod/create-deco), industrial decoration themed mod
- [Create Interiors](https://modrinth.com/mod/interiors), adds furniture to the Create mod
- [More Composter Variants](https://modrinth.com/mod/more-composter-variants), adds composter variants in different types of wood
- [More Feeding Trough Variants](https://modrinth.com/mod/more-composter-variants), adds feeding trough variants in different types of wood

**Other**
- [Death Finder](https://modrinth.com/mod/death-finder), shows the place you last died
- [Project Save the Pets](https://modrinth.com/mod/projectsavethepets), prevents you or other players from hurting your pets
- [Spawners+](https://modrinth.com/mod/spawners_plus), adds craftable spawners
- [Waystones](https://modrinth.com/mod/waystones), adds craftable waystones to make travel across the world easier

**Dependencies**
- [Architectury](https://modrinth.com/mod/architectury-api)
- [Balm](https://modrinth.com/mod/balm)
- [BCLib](https://modrinth.com/mod/bclib)
- [Cardinal Components API](https://modrinth.com/mod/cardinal-components-api)
- [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api)
- [Fabric Language Kotlin](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-language-kotlin)
- [GeckoLib 4](https://modrinth.com/mod/geckolib)
- [Puzzles Lib](https://modrinth.com/mod/puzzles-lib)
- [Quad](https://modrinth.com/mod/quad)
- [TerraBlender](https://modrinth.com/mod/terrablender)
- [YUNG's API](https://modrinth.com/mod/yungs-api)
